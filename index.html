Hi, I'm Ilaria!

Who I am

I was born and raised in the province of Milan (Italy), but with a curious and explorer soul. At the age of 11 I made my first trip abroad and from that 2006 onwards I haven't stopped since.

Interests

Travelling makes me feel alive, makes me escape, and for me it is essential to have an open mind and be receptive to external stimuli . The most significant destinations so far have been South Africa, New Zealand (where I lived for four months), New York and Colombia.

Travelling is not my only passion: I have also always enjoyed drawing, creating and exercising. At the same time, I am attracted to everything that has to do with logic and reasoning, such as crosswords, puzzles and board games.

Education

My passion for travelling led me to take up language studies, first at linguistic high school and then at the undergraduate degree course (foreign languages applied to international relations). Over time I have been able to study and improve English, French, Spanish and German , but I would also like to learn a 'non-European' language...who knows what will be next!

In 2018 I then decided that I wanted to apply my knowledge to a more specific field, so I enrolled in the master's faculty of communication and marketing. Despite the difficult covid period, I graduated in March 2021 with a dissertation on cryptocurrencies and the digital world . A few weeks later, I found a job at a leading food industry company in Milan.

In May of the same year, I started my internship at Fratelli Pagani S.p.A., which soon evolved into a permanent contract. There I deal with all-round communication, B2B and digital marketing, and the organisation of national and international trade fairs and events.
